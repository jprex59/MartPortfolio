What is Fluxy ?

Fluxy is an auto checkout software that allows you to buy pairs in quantity on different European sites. The interface has been designed to be as simple as possible, the application supports proxies, success webhooks and has many bypasses for protections like Datadome or cloudflare.

How i made Fluxy ?

The idea for Fluxy came to me 1 year ago, but I don't have any coding skills and that's what delayed me a lot. So I looked for a developer who accepted my financing terms, that is 50% of the Fluxy company. It was not easy, but in December I found a competent developer, we are now a partner. He is in charge of the development part of the application and I manage the commercial and management part.
