Mart01

MART01 is a simple solution to wear the most limited sneakers possible. We offer all types of models crossing different brands such as adidas, nike and new balance. We also offer a buyback service, so you can sell your pairs easily for a quick cashout.

How it’s started

It started on February 7th, 2019. With a friend we bought our first pair, an air max 90 off white. We made a profit of $ 350. So I then continued to educate myself and learn how to develop this idea, and have more and more shoes.

How it’s going

Now MART01 is a real company, which does limited purchase and resale of shoes. We no longer just buy from official sites like Nike, now we also buy pairs from individuals, providing them with a fast and reliable sales solution.
